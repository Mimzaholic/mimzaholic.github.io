Wanna get into some industry?

OldGuyMonty has made his library of BPOs and Reactions accesible to the entire corp. Anyone with Scientific Networking can reach out from a distance and assist with researching BPOs, make copies, or run jobs and use the corporate Production wallet to pay for it.

Munory - Glory to the Builders (Control Tower Alt Delete) Is our main industry location, but you can build anywhere. Those of you who partake in industry can make copies of my BPOs and use them for doing industry. Both personal and allaince contract. Just please do not abuse the privilage. (See Below)

Use of the BPOs and Reactions will be done by placing a container in the new hangar with your player name on it and putting your build materials inside (ask OldGuyMonty to rename or add a container for you). You can then make a BPO copy to place in your container and then run the job to and from there while using the corp production wallet to pay for it.

The Production wallet is limited so please consider donating back to it from time to time to help keep it running.

We monitor both the Corp industry logs and the Production wallet for abuse, so please use the honor system when utilizing both. The idea here is participation and getting more people involved in industry. For those not interested in industry, please conside donating reasearch slot time to help out. Those that don't do industry, but use their 1 or 2 research slots for doing long research jobs on teh BPOs are helping immensely. And with the production wallet to pay for it you're not losing anything.

- First and foremost keep in mind, these BPOs and Reaction Formulas are the personal property of OldGuyMonty. They are not assets owned by the Corporation.

- Please make a copy (or copies) of a BPO and have them output to your personal corporate build container. This way the BPO frees up for somone else to utilize. If you are making multiple copies, Please be sure to notify OldGuyMonty and for what purpose. The BPOs and Reaction Formulas are being provided for everyone to build with, not for continuous copying for personal revenue generation. Abuse will lead to losing access privelages.

Build Materials - You are responsible for your own build materials. As it will be a community hangar, all containers will be accesible by everyone. I recommend utilizing secure audit container to monitor inputs and outputs or only keeping what is needed for a particular build. Use at your own risk. To utilize a secure container, select the items in the container, right click them and select "Lock Item". The items can still be unlocked and taken by anybody, but the audit log will tell you WHO did it. NOTE: Theft from other players' container(s) will result in getting kicked from the Corporation.

Build/Research Fees - The Corporation has made the Production wallet accessible to all for use in paying research and build fees. This is not a bottomless wallet and will periodically be added to if funds are available, but members are also asked to consider donating to the wallet to keep the magic going. NOTE: Theft from this wallet will result in getting kicked from the Corporation.

- If you have empty/unused Industry research slots PLEASE consider donating their usage to helping research teh BPOs that OldGuyMonty has made available. Ultimately this benefits the corporation and you can use the Production wallet to pay the fees.

BPO Gaps/Addition/Donation - If demand for a BPO becomes large or if there is a BPO that you would like to see added to the collection speak with OldGuyMonty. Consideration will be given (if funds are available) to him purchasing extra copies for people to research and utilize

- The ability exists to deposit Blueprints into the collection as well (you will not be able to take them back out once they are put in) If there is a BPO gap that you would like to fill (you have a duplicate or are willing to purchase and donate) I will be happy to accept it into my collection. If you prefer that it go in as a Corp owned BPO make sure to let me know and I will add another hangar for corp owned BPOs. We have a few, but not many. NOTE: This is only for those rare generous souls and should, in no way, be considered a solicitation. (I"m answering the question before it can be asked)

BPC's (Blueprint Copies)

- There is also a folder of BPC's available for all to use. These are mostly the daily freebie blueprints, but there are occasional gems that end up in there is someone no longer needs a blueprint. Feel free to give and take at will from this container, just be courteous and only take what you intend to utilize.

Slot containers (Salvage)

- There are numerous containers available labeled for High/Mid/Low Slot. This is all salvage that has been collected and donated from mission running. This is to be used for outfitting throw-away ships for mining, testing fits, learning PVP, and so on. If you tend to collect everything and don't know what to do with it, consider donating it to the corp and sharing for others to use.

- Actual Salvage Materials like Alloyed Tritanium Bar , Burned Logic Circuit , and Fried Interface Circuit can be donated to OldGuyMonty for use in building station rigs if you have no use for them.
